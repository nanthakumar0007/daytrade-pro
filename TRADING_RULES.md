## ⏰ TIME-BASED STRATEGY SELECTION

### 🌅 BEFORE 8:00 AM CST → INTRADAY STRATEGY
- Buy at market open (9:30 AM)
- Sell during the day (9:30 AM - 3:55 PM)
- Hold time: 1-4 hours
- Goal: Quick $1 profit from momentum
- Risk: Market can gap against you at open

### 🌙 AFTER 11:00 AM CST → SWING STRATEGY
- Buy at market close (3:30 PM - 4:00 PM)
- Sell next morning at open (9:30 AM - 10:30 AM)
- Hold time: ~18 hours
- Goal: Capture overnight gap or next day momentum
- Risk: Overnight gap can blow through stop loss

### ⚡ ANY TIME → SAME-DAY OPTIONS
- Buy 0 DTE option (expires today)
- Must sell SAME DAY before market close
- Hold time: Minutes to hours
- Goal: Quick $0.50-$2.00 profit on premium
- Risk: Can lose 100% of money paid immediately

### 📅 FRIDAY ONLY → WEEKLY OPTIONS
- Buy Friday weekly option
- Hold through weekend
- Sell Monday morning
- Hold time: ~60 hours (OVERNIGHT RISK!)
- Goal: Weekend gap / gamma play = $0.50-$3.00
- Risk: EXTREME - gap risk, 100% loss possible

## 🛑 STOP LOSS DISCIPLINE (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

**Rules:**
- [ ] Enter stop loss order IMMEDIATELY after buying
- [ ] NEVER move stop loss lower (only higher if stock moves in your favor)
- [ ] NEVER override stop loss manually
- [ ] ALWAYS close at stop if triggered (no "one more minute" hoping)
- [ ] If stopped out, DO NOT buy the same stock again that day

**Psychological Truth:**
- Small losses keep you in the game
- Large losses end your account
- Discipline > Hope

---

## 🎁 TAKE PROFIT DISCIPLINE

**Rules:**
- [ ] Close position AT profit target (not "wait for more")
- [ ] If profit target hit early in day, TAKE IT
- [ ] Greed turns winners into losers
- [ ] $1 profit is $1 profit (congratulate yourself)
- [ ] More trades > bigger trades

**Why Close at Target:**
- You already have 2:1 RRR
- Probability decreases the longer you hold
- Next setup is around the corner
- This is "mechanical" trading (no emotions)

## 🚨 RED FLAGS - CANCEL TRADE IF ANY APPLY

- [ ] Technical indicator was NOT actually met (recheck RSI/volume)
- [ ] Stock price already at my profit target (missed the entry)
- [ ] Federal Reserve or major economic news today (avoid volatility)
- [ ] Stock just had earnings yesterday (IV crush / gap risk)
- [ ] App recommendation RSI is outside 45-65 (recalc or wait)
- [ ] Market is down 2%+ at open (risk-off day, fewer setups)
- [ ] You don't have $35 available capital left
- [ ] Brokerage is down / slow to execute

## 🔐 ACCOUNT PROTECTION RULES

1. **Never use margin** (borrowed money) - stick to cash
2. **Never YOLO** (all-in) - follow the position sizing rules
3. **Never revenge trade** - if you lose, skip next setup to cool off
4. **Never trade on emotion** - mechanical = profitable
5. **Never ignore stops** - they exist for a reason
6. **Never add to losing trades** - one entry, one exit
7. **Never trade illiquid stocks** - volume must be high (>1.5x)
8. **Never panic sell** - if you hit stop loss, exit; don't hold hoping
9. **Never skip the plan** - follow the 2:1 RRR every time
10. **Never risk more than -$0.50 per trade** - this is THE rule

## 🎓 WHAT TO DO AFTER 10 TRADES

Review your trading journal:
1. What's my win rate? (Should be 50%+)
2. Are stops hitting too early? (Adjust volatility buffer)
3. Are targets not reaching? (Adjust true range multiple)
4. Am I breaking any rules? (Fix immediately)
5. Is the strategy working? (Keep going or pivot)

**Key metric: Win rate × Avg Win > Loss rate × Avg Loss**

Example: 60% win rate × $1 gain > 40% loss rate × $0.50 loss = PROFITABLE
